Dundein dairy is a local store that sepcialises in everything dairy. Sourced from healthy cows and happy farmers, Dundein Dairy only uses the freshest milk to produce their high quality goods for you to purchase at any time! We take pride in our products and our services, as one of New Zealand's top dairy services available. Our online store is open 24/7 and we provide quick, contactless, deliveries to ensure that the products you purchase are as good as when they leave our facility and to keep our drivers and other people in the community safe.
